Step into My Office Makes it easy to share notes

and homework with other students!

Step into My Office, is the note and exams sharing web app for high school and college students. Login or Register and join your school and class to begin sharing your past notes, exams, quizzes or homework assignments! You can also chat with other students. Learning is easier when we learn together!
